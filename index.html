About Us

This is what we stand for

Professional Development

We aim to provide the skills needed to succeed in interviews and in the work place such as how to manage our personal queer identities and workplace relationships

Education

We aim to educate our members about the different industries that we can enter when we leave college. This includes: Healthcare, Government, Consulting

Community

We aim to be an inclusive and diverse community. This means that the McDonough Alliance isn’t just for students in the McDonough School of Business, but also students in the College, NHS, and SFS.

Resumé Workshops

Helping undergraduate students create their own resumés

We got together with undergraduate students to help them create their own resumés so that they leave an impression with prospective employers and on-campus organisations

  • Target Audience: Undergraduates
  • Category: Professional Development

Networking Events

Connecting with companies and organisations that are looking for queer undergraduates

We met with some large companies who had recieved our resumé book so they could get to know the our members face to face

  • Target Audience: Undergraduates
  • Category: Networking

Office Visits

Visiting local firms

Learn about the professional life of many different industries from D.C. firms

  • Target Audience: Undergraduates
  • Category: Professional Development

CAB Fair

Come meet with us in person at CAB Fair

Come say to us at Georgetown University’s CAB Fair! We’ll have lots of free stuff to give away and lots of information on becoming a member of The McDonough Alliance

  • Target Audience: Undergraduates
  • Category: Membership

Coffee Chats

Connect with those at the LGBTQI+ Resource center on campus through coffee chats

Get to know the resources we have available on campus through the LGBTQI+ Resource center! Coffee chats are held regularly throughout the semester and provide students a chance to express their thoughts and feelings about their own personal journey in a judgement free environment!

  • Target Audience: Undergraduates
  • Category: Social

Weekly Meetings

Join us for our weekly meetings

Weekly meetings are where we come together as a community to learn about the different opportunities available to us, as well as provide support to those of us who need it. Light refreshments are always served.

  • Target Audience: Undergraduates
  • Category: Membership
